Rugby Competition Ranking

In this project, I attempted to use data from Its Rugby to create a ranking of the relative strengths of a handful of international leagues. To do this, I built a predictive model that can predict whether a player's minutes would increase after a change in leagues. I ran this model on a dataset of semi-random fake players and ranked the leagues based on how often the players' minutes are predicted to increase. I collected the relevant data using Beautiful Soup to parse HTML code from Its Rugby.
